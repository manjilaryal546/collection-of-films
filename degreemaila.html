Degree Maila (MA 3rd CLASS)

Comedy, Drama, Romance | 2h 30m | 13 Chaitra,2080


PLOT

"Degree Maila" unfolds as a captivating comic tale that delves into the life of a man on a quest to find his place in society while longing for love and recognition. Amidst a whirlwind of trials and errors, he revolts against societal expectations and surrenders to personal aspirations that define his journey.
